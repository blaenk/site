**Problem**: Given end-points $[a, b)$ where $a$ is not necessarily 0, determine the mid-point.

**Solution**: Subtract the endpoints and divide them by two to get the middle, then shift the middle to $a$.

<div>
$$\frac {(b - a)} 2 + a$$
</div>
